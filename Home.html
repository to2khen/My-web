About Us

SKT T1, now known as T1, is a legendary South Korean esports team famous for its dominance in League of Legends. They hold the record for the most World Championship wins, claiming titles in 2013, 2015, and 2016, and are the only team to win Worlds three times. Known for their tactical brilliance and resilience, SKT T1 became synonymous with greatness under the leadership of Faker, widely regarded as the greatest LoL player of all time. Although rebranded to T1 in 2019, the team's legacy as a pioneer of excellence in esports remains unmatched.
